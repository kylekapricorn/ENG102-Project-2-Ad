Let's protect Mobile Bay

The city of Mobile has hesitated to fully commit to growing its tourism infrastructure. Taking initiative and investing in a way for Mobile and the greater area to stand on its own for tourism will secure the lively hood of both the local ecosystem and lifestyle of the residents. This area has so much diversity to offer and should be protected for future generations as well as the nation.

Preservation

Ecotourism provides sustainable income that directly aides the preservation of land.

Economy

Creating a destination of the South will create a thriving tourism sector for the Bay area.

Lifestyle

Mobile has a rich, 300+ year history and a strong identity with the brakish waters.

Mobile Bay, Amazon of America

The creation of an ecotourism centered park is the key to the survival and prosperity of the local area. This plan will protect the biodiversity of the area, stimulate the local economy with tourism, and ensure that the identity of Mobilians can be preserved. A space like this would become a major destination of the Gulf. Being considered the ‘Amazon of America’, the biggest pull would be the amazing range of flora and fauna that the Bay has to offer. From the sandy-white beaches to the marsh to downtown.

The Bay is Worth Protecting

Creating something as important as a protected park takes a tremendous amount of work and time. Investing efforts into this plan will have tremendous payoffs for the city and the ecosystem.
